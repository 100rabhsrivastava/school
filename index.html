About Us


Gyanveer English School was established in 2016 by Late Rajendralal Memorial Education Society (LRMES), Reg.No F-0034168(NGP) a trust which has been involved in community service for over four years. More than 4 years of fruitful journey has helped GES grow deep roots and establish a strong foundation in primary education. GES mainly focuses on the educationally backward region of Vidarbha and aims to impart the global way of education to the students in this area...
